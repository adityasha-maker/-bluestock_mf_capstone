# Day 3 EDA Summary

## Dataset Overview

* NAV History Records: 46,000
* Investor Transaction Records: 32,778
* Scheme Performance Records: 40

## Descriptive Statistics

The NAV dataset contains 46,000 observations with an average NAV of 269.57. NAV values range from 26.14 to 4268.55, indicating significant variation across mutual fund schemes.

The investor transaction dataset contains 32,778 records with an average transaction amount of ₹107,437. The average investor annual income is ₹26.18 lakh.

The scheme performance dataset contains 40 mutual fund schemes. The average 1-year return is 14.38%, average 3-year return is 14.09%, and average 5-year return is 14.52%. The average AUM is ₹26,091 crore and average Morningstar rating is 4.25.

## Category Analysis

Large Cap funds dominate the dataset with 14 schemes, followed by Mid Cap (7) and Small Cap (6). Other categories include Liquid, Gilt, Flexi Cap, Value, Index, and ELSS funds.

## Risk Analysis

Moderate risk schemes are the most common with 16 schemes. High-risk schemes account for 8 funds, while Very High and Low risk categories each contain 6 funds.

## Return Analysis

Small Cap funds generated the highest average 3-year return of 21.69%, followed by Mid Cap funds at 16.59% and Flexi Cap funds at 15.50%. Debt-oriented categories such as Liquid and Gilt funds delivered comparatively lower returns.

## Investor Demographics

The largest investor segment belongs to the 26–35 age group with 13,463 investors, followed by the 36–45 age group. Male investors (21,809) significantly outnumber female investors (10,969).

## Geographic Analysis

Punjab recorded the highest number of transactions (2,965), followed closely by Madhya Pradesh (2,931), Tamil Nadu (2,806), Gujarat (2,780), and West Bengal (2,748).

## Conclusion

The analysis shows that Large Cap funds are the most prevalent category, while Small Cap funds offer the highest returns. Most investors belong to the 26–35 age group and Moderate-risk schemes dominate the portfolio universe. These insights will be used for visualization and dashboard development in the next phase.
