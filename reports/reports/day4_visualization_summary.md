# Day 4 – Data Visualization Summary

## Objective

The objective of Day 4 was to visualize mutual fund and investor data using Matplotlib and Seaborn.

## Visualizations Created

### 1. Average 3-Year Return by Category

A bar chart was created to compare average 3-year returns across mutual fund categories. Small Cap funds generated the highest average returns, followed by Mid Cap and Flexi Cap funds.

### 2. Risk Distribution

A count plot was developed to analyze risk grades across schemes. Moderate risk schemes represented the largest segment of the dataset.

### 3. Investor Age Group Distribution

An age distribution chart showed that investors in the 26–35 age group constitute the majority of mutual fund investors.

### 4. Gender Distribution

A pie chart illustrated investor gender distribution. Male investors represented approximately two-thirds of all investors.

## Key Insights

* Small Cap funds delivered the highest average 3-year returns.
* Moderate risk funds dominate the portfolio universe.
* Investors aged 26–35 form the largest investor segment.
* Male participation exceeds female participation in the dataset.

## Outcome

Four professional visualizations were generated and saved in the reports folder for future dashboard development and business analysis.
